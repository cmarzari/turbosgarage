What We Offer
Basic Reprogramming Service- $30 (Return shipping included!)

Mix and match any of the services below for one flat rate!

Speedometer Calibration - Recalibrate your speedometer for changes in tire size and rear end gear ratio. Please include all relevant information such as original tire size/gear ratio, current tire size/gear ratio, and if possible, actual speedometer discrepancy.

VATS security system disable- Disable the stock Vehicle Anti-Theft System (VATS, or PASS-Key). This system will disable the fuel pump and starter solenoid if it detects the wrong ignition key. However, worn ignition switches and keys, as well as wiring issues, can cause this system to malfunction, potentially leaving you stranded. Deleting this system from your pcm will render the system useless, and incapable of interfering with your car’s engine.

Cooling Fan Temperatures- Command your cooling fans to turn on at a lower temperature (please specify). Turning fans on earlier can help prevent overheating, better A/C performance, and lower engine temperatures in traffic.

Diagnostic Codes- Disable diagnostic codes from displaying on dash. Note: this will only keep the check engine light from turning on, and not as a repair measure.

Traction Control - Disable traction control (F and D bodies). Use at own risk!
